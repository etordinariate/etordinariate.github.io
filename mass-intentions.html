Mass Schedule

Please note: There is a short delay between the scheduling of intentions and when these pages are updated, and changes to the schedule may happen at any moment without notice.

Public mass intentions are limited to registered and active members of Ordinariate Members of East Tennessee.

Please indicate your preferred time, and we will generally try to meet that request as close as possible in light of all the other requests. The schedule for certain masses throughout the year may be adjusted as varying times are determined for special occasions, extra masses and seasonal changes. So please review the schedule which is updated periodically.

On the mass intention schedule one can likewise see whether it will be a sung mass and whether there will be confessions during the mass.

This mass intention request form is intended for registered and active members of the Ordinariate Members of East Tennessee. We kindly ask that others refrain from submitting requests.
